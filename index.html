About Me

Mohammed Liton Ali is a Bangladeshi information professional serving as Assistant Cataloguer at Islamic University of Technology (IUT) and Liaison Officer at the university's International Affairs Office. He specializes in digital library systems, cataloguing operations, and global academic partnerships while supporting international students with comprehensive integration services.
