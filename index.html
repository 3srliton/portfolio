I'm a researcher, educator, and creative professional passionate about exploring the intersections of digital humanities, AI ethics, data, and community engagement. I am currently pursuing my Master of Arts (Digital Humanities) at the University of Alberta, where I combine academic research, technology, and creativity. My journey began with a Bachelor's in Information Science & Library Management at the University of Dhaka, which gave me strong foundations in knowledge systems, digital tools, and archival practices.

I've worked in academia, NGOs, and cultural institutions, developing expertise in data analysis, digital knowledge management, and community engagement. My creative edge lies in graphic design, visualization, and 2D/3D modeling, making me passionate about bridging technical and artistic perspectives.
